jaypha.inet.mime.contentdisposition

A structure for MIME Content-Disposition headers.

The MIME content disposition header is defined in RFC2183, updated by RFC2231.

The MIME content disposition provides extra information for the handling of the data, and is typically included as part of mulitpart documents. For example, it can specify whether the data is a part of the larger document (inline), separate (attachment), or part of a form submission (form-data).

MIME content disposition headers can also provide extra information in the form of parameters. The parameters are given in the format of <attribute>=<value>, separated by ";". The value can be in quotes and comments are allowed as per RFC2822.

Example

Content-Disposition: form-data; name="icon"; filename="arrow.png"

The data is part of a form submission. There are two parameters: "name" and "filename" with values "icon" and "arrow.png" respectively.

struct MimeContentDisposition

MIME Content-Disposition.

Use this for creating/reading/storing information relevant to Content-Disposition MIME headers.

Use extractMimeContentDispoisition to read form input, and toMimeHeader to prepare for output.
